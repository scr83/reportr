## Click 1: Connect Google Search Console {#connect-search-console}

Google Search Console contains the most critical SEO data: what keywords you're ranking for, how often you appear in search results, and how many people click through to your site.

**Step-by-Step:**

1. **Click "Connect Search Console"** in your reporting dashboard

2. **Select your Google account** when prompted (or sign in)

3. **Click "Allow"** to grant read-only access to Search Console data

That's it. The system will automatically pull data for all verified properties in your Search Console account.

**What Gets Connected:**

- Search performance data (queries, pages, countries, devices)
- Coverage data (which pages are indexed)
- Enhancement data (mobile usability, Core Web Vitals)
- Security issues and manual actions

**Security Note**: You're granting read-only access. The automation tool cannot make changes to your Search Console settings or data.

## Troubleshooting Common Issues {#troubleshooting}

**"No Data Available" Errors:**

- **Check permissions**: Make sure you granted access to all requested properties
- **Verify date ranges**: Some accounts have limited historical data
- **Wait for data**: New websites may not have enough data for meaningful reports

**"Connection Failed" Messages:**

- **Re-authorize**: Google tokens expire; simply reconnect your accounts
- **Check property access**: Ensure you have proper permissions in Search Console/Analytics
- **Clear browser cache**: Sometimes old cookies interfere with OAuth

**"Report Generation Failed":**

- **Try smaller date range**: Large date ranges can timeout
- **Check data sources**: One source might be temporarily unavailable
- **Contact support**: Most platforms offer free setup assistance

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How secure is connecting my Google accounts?**
All connections use Google's official OAuth2 protocol with read-only access. Your login credentials are never stored, and you can revoke access anytime from your Google Account settings.

**Can I customize what data appears in reports?**
Yes, most automation platforms allow you to choose which metrics to include, exclude certain data points, and customize the report structure to match your clients' needs.

**What happens if Google changes their APIs?**
Reputable automation tools monitor for API changes and update their integrations automatically. You shouldn't experience any disruption in your report generation.

**Can I still create manual reports when needed?**
Absolutely. Automation doesn't replace your ability to create custom analysis. It handles the routine monthly reporting while freeing you to focus on strategic insights when clients need them.

**How often does the data update?**
Search Console and Analytics data typically updates within 24-48 hours. PageSpeed Insights is tested weekly to avoid rate limiting. You always get the most recent available data when generating reports.
