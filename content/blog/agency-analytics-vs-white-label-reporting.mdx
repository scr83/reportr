## What is Agency Analytics?

[Agency Analytics](https://agencyanalytics.com/) is an all-in-one reporting platform designed for full-service agencies managing multiple marketing channels. Founded in 2012 and based in Canada, it's become one of the most recognized names in agency reporting software.

**Key features include:**

- SEO reporting and keyword tracking

- PPC campaign management and reporting

- Social media analytics across platforms

- Email marketing campaign tracking

- Over 50 integrations with marketing tools

- White-label capability with custom branding options

- Team collaboration features for agencies with multiple users

**Pricing ranges from $99-$249/month per user**, making it a significant investment for agencies primarily focused on SEO services. The platform is built for agencies that manage comprehensive digital marketing campaigns across multiple channels for their clients.
